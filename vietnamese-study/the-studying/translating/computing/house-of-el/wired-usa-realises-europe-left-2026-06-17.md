If a noun has a passive verb used as its adjective, the adjectival phrase goes after the noun:&#x20;

<mark style="background-color:red;">confirmed</mark> <mark style="background-color:green;">cases</mark> <mark style="background-color:yellow;">of</mark> <mark style="background-color:blue;">storms</mark>&#x20;

<mark style="background-color:green;">cases</mark> <mark style="background-color:yellow;">(of)</mark> <mark style="background-color:blue;">storms</mark> <mark style="background-color:red;">be-confirm</mark>
