# Abjoid Conscript & AbjAng

## Abjoid Script

"_Abjoid"_ is just a name for this script while I am developing it.

The _abj_ part comes from the word _abjad_, a script class including the Arabic and Hebrew scripts. My conscript isn't really an abjad, but it is visually inspired by Arabic, and has a few similar features.

The _oid_ part comes from the suffix _-oid_, meaning "alike in form".



## AbjAng Orthography

_"AbjAng"_ is the name of the orthographic transcription method for writing English in this conscript.&#x20;

The _ang_ part comes from _Anglo_, that is _English_, hence why it would be a misnomer to use _ang_ in the script name.

As most orthographic transcription methods are somewhat deleterious, the majority of my own conscript orthographies are based upon my own NewEng Orthography, a lossless English orthography.
